Event Overview & Schedule

Welcome to the premier maritime events on the East Coast. For over five decades, the Annapolis Boat Shows have transformed the historic City Dock into the world's largest in-water display of boats and marine equipment. These internationally acclaimed shows attract tens of thousands of boating enthusiasts, industry professionals, and maritime adventurers to the sailing capital of America.

US Powerboat Show

October 2-5, 2025

The United States Powerboat Show features the finest selection of powerboats, from luxurious motor yachts to high-performance fishing vessels. City Dock's floating docks create a temporary marina showcasing hundreds of boats in an unparalleled waterfront setting.

Featured Boat Categories

Luxury Motor Yachts

Sunseeker, Viking, Azimut, and other premium manufacturers showcasing their latest flagship models with cutting-edge design and technology.

Sport Fishing Vessels

Center consoles, offshore fishing boats, and tournament-ready vessels from leading manufacturers specializing in angling excellence.

Cruisers & Trawlers

Nordic Tugs, family cruisers, and long-distance trawlers perfect for coastal exploration and Great Loop adventures.

Performance Boats

High-speed vessels, Nimbus performance cruisers, and recreational boats designed for thrill-seekers and weekend adventures.

Highlighted Exhibitors

  • Seattle Yachts: Featuring award-winning Nimbus Boats and Nordic Tugs (Dock D)
  • Sunseeker USA: Luxury performance motor yachts with cutting-edge design
  • Sunsail: Award-winning charter yachts and vacation planning services
  • Leading Outboard Manufacturers: Mercury, Yamaha, and Honda marine engines
  • Electronics & Navigation: Garmin, Raymarine, and Furuno marine systems
  • Marine Accessories: Comprehensive selection of boating gear and equipment

🚤 Featured Debut: 2025 Nimbus W-11

Experience the stunning 40-foot weekender from Nimbus Boats featuring award-winning Scandinavian design, walkaround decks, folding side balconies, and twin Mercury outboards capable of 30-50 knot speeds. Perfect for coastal adventures and entertaining.

Educational Opportunities

Attend expert-led seminars on powerboat handling, marine electronics, fishing techniques, and coastal navigation. Industry professionals offer hands-on demonstrations and personalized consultations throughout the show.

US Sailboat Show

October 9-12, 2025

The United States Sailboat Show is the world's largest in-water sailboat exhibition, featuring floating docks lined with sailboats from manufacturers around the globe. This internationally acclaimed event serves seasoned sailors and newcomers alike with the latest in sailing technology and maritime innovation.

Boat Categories on Display

Monohull Cruisers

Performance cruisers, blue-water sailboats, and family-friendly vessels designed for comfort and seaworthiness in all conditions.

Multihulls

Catamarans and trimarans offering speed, stability, and spacious accommodations for coastal and offshore sailing.

Racing Sailboats

High-performance racing yachts, one-design classes, and competition vessels built for speed and precision.

Sailing Dinghies

Small boats perfect for learning, day sailing, and recreational activities in protected waters.

2025 Gear Innovations

  • Easysea Flipper: Revolutionary foldable winch handle that locks directly onto winch
  • Mustang Survival Gear: New PFAS-free offshore foul-weather gear
  • OGM LX LED Lighting: Dual-purpose steaming/deck lights from Weems & Plath
  • Arntson Marine WindSun Hat: Designed to withstand 70 mph winds
  • Advanced Solar Panels: Shade-tolerant panels for enhanced power generation
  • Navigation Electronics: Latest GPS, radar, and communication equipment

Educational Resources

Cruising Education

The Boat Galley launches new courses for aspiring cruisers, covering everything from provisioning to offshore passages.

Weather & Navigation

Carolyn Shearlock's new book "Weather Basics for Boaters" provides essential guidance for informed weather decision-making.

⛵ Professional Development

Connect with charter companies, sailing schools, and industry professionals. Explore career opportunities in the marine industry and expand your sailing knowledge through expert-led workshops and demonstrations.

Money-Saving Tips

  • Watch for exhibitor promotional codes (up to 30% savings)
  • Follow social media accounts for flash ticket giveaways
  • Check with marine industry partners for complimentary passes
  • Military discount available on final Sunday of each show
  • Children 6 and under attend free with paid adult admission
  • Purchase tickets online in advance for digital convenience

Group Sales & Corporate Packages

Special rates available for yacht clubs, corporate groups, and marine industry organizations. Contact the show organizers directly for volume pricing and corporate hospitality packages.

💡 Pro Tip: Preview Days

While preview days cost more, they offer significant advantages: fewer crowds, more personal attention from exhibitors, better photo opportunities, and first access to exclusive boat debuts and special offers.

Professional Navigator's Tips

Maximizing Your Show Experience

🗓️ Pre-Show Planning

• Download show apps and maps
• Research target boats/exhibitors
• Schedule appointments with dealers
• Check weather forecasts

👟 Day-of Preparation

• Wear comfortable walking shoes
• Bring sunscreen and water
• Charge phone/camera batteries
• Carry business cards

Industry Professional Benefits

  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with manufacturers, dealers, and fellow professionals
  • Product Launches: First access to new boat models and marine technology
  • Industry Seminars: Technical sessions and market trend discussions
  • Trade Meetings: Schedule private appointments with key suppliers
  • Competitive Intelligence: Assess market positioning and pricing strategies
  • Education Credits: Some seminars offer continuing education credits

Technology & Innovation Focus

🔋 Sustainable Technology

Electric propulsion systems, solar panels, and eco-friendly marine products showcase the industry's environmental commitment.

📱 Digital Integration

Smart boat systems, IoT connectivity, and advanced navigation technology represent the future of boating.

Essential Contacts

  • Emergency Services: Anne Arundel Medical Center

🎯 Strategic Approach

Dedicate time strategically: Preview days for serious shopping, weekend days for networking and education. Consider attending both shows to fully appreciate the complete spectrum of recreational boating.

Post-Show Follow-Up

📋 Documentation

Organize brochures, business cards, and photos. Create comparison charts for boats and equipment of interest.

🤝 Relationship Building

Follow up promptly with new contacts. Schedule sea trials and further discussions while interest is high.

⚓ Final Note

The Annapolis Boat Shows represent more than a commercial event—they celebrate the maritime heritage and future innovation that define American boating culture. Whether you're expanding your fleet, upgrading equipment, or exploring new horizons, these shows provide unparalleled access to the marine industry's finest offerings.
